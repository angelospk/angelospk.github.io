# WhyPride.gr

**An interactive guide on why Pride matters**

## Overview

WhyPride.gr is a calm, well-documented interactive site that tries to bring the LGBTQ+
community closer to people who don't know it from the inside — not people who hate, just
people who've never been in a position to understand why Pride exists.

### The Idea

Instead of walls of text and arguments, the site uses small interactive challenges: you flip
a card, answer a scenario, play with a switch. Some make a newcomer stop and think, others
tell someone who already knows something new. Along the way it answers a few questions most
people never say out loud.

## Key Features

- **Interactive challenges:** short, hands-on prompts instead of a lecture.
- **Ally quiz:** reflects back how much of an ally you actually are.
- **Calm, documented tone:** explains why Pride exists in a grounded, accessible way.
