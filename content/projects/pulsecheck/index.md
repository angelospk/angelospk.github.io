# PulseCheck

**Anonymous employee pulse surveys & insights — Founder**

## Overview

PulseCheck measures the real pulse of a company's workforce through genuinely anonymous
surveys and insights an HR team can act on. It's aimed at EU mid-market companies whose
people have stopped answering the usual engagement surveys.

### The Problem

Employees don't trust that engagement surveys are actually anonymous, so participation and
honesty stay low. HR ends up with data it can't act on, and the people who are about to
leave are the ones who say the least.

### The Approach

Build the product around credible anonymity first, then turn the answers into clear signals
instead of dashboards nobody opens. The goal is for HR to see what's really going on early
enough to do something about it.

## What I'm doing

As founder, I lead product, positioning, and engineering. That means deciding what the
anonymity guarantee actually is, building the survey and insight side, and talking to HR
people about whether it would change how they work.
